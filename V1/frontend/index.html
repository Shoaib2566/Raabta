Our Story

Born from a real problem, built for everyone.

It started with a friendship. When Muhammad's best friend left Pakistan for Italy to pursue his studies, the distance didn't make the responsibilities back home disappear — if anything, they multiplied.

"Every few days, another call. 'Yaar, can you pay the LESCO bill?' 'Can you check on my mother?' 'The plumber never showed up.' It was manageable at first — until it wasn't."

Muhammad was happy to help. But he was also a student, with his own life to manage. And as he looked around, he realized this wasn't just his friend's problem — it was the story of millions of Pakistanis abroad, silently depending on people back home to keep things running.

That realization became Raabta. A platform where the distance doesn't matter, where a request placed from London or Dubai gets handled in Lahore or Islamabad — professionally, transparently, and on time.
